Plugins

6 notification sources, one tool

Connect the services you already use. Each plugin fetches in the background and surfaces what matters.

GH
GitHub
PR reviews, CI failures, mentions, and issue assignments delivered as they happen.
SL
Slack
DMs, channel mentions, and thread replies without switching windows.
X
X / Twitter
Mentions, DMs, and bookmark highlights from your timeline.
EM
Email
Unread count and important subject lines from your inbox via IMAP.
$$
Stocks / Crypto
BTC, ETH, AAPL, and any ticker you track -- with price change alerts.
MC
MCP Bridge
Pull notifications from connected MCP servers. No API key needed.
Surfaces

5 ways to see your notifications

From subtle spinners to full dashboards. Every surface is configurable and priority-aware.

1
Spinner Verbs
Notification titles replace the Claude Code spinner text while it thinks. A passive, ambient way to stay informed without any interruption.
2
Status Line
The top-priority notification is shown in a persistent status bar with clickable links that open in your browser.
3
Context Injection
High-priority items are injected into Claude's context so it can mention things naturally. "By the way, your CI is failing on line 42."
4
Session Summary
When you start or resume a session, see a digest of everything you missed -- PRs, messages, price moves, and more.
5
Dashboard
A full notification list via vibenotifications dashboard -- filterable, sortable, with all the details.
